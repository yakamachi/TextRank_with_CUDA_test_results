Second Clown I tell thee she is: and therefore make her grave straight: the crowner hath sat on her, and finds it Christian burial. First Clown 'Tis a quick lie, sir; 'twill away gain, from me to you. First Clown Ay, tell me that, and unyoke. QUEEN GERTRUDE For love of God, forbear him. I'll do't. LAERTES Must there no more be done? Imperious Caesar, dead and turn'd to clay, Might stop a hole to keep the wind away: O, that that earth, which kept the world in awe, Should patch a wall to expel the winter flaw! Second Clown But is this law? Go, get thee to Yaughan: fetch me a stoup of liquor.
